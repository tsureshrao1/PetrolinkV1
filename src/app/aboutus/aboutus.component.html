ABOUT US

Petro Link Holding Company KSCC (PLH) was Established in 2007. THe Company has an authorized Capital of KD 25,000,000/-

The company has exploited the growing markets opportunity for energies by taking equity positions, acquisitions, joint ventures, and by participating in green-field projects. PLH has established peer to peer connection with all of its subsidiaries to maintain a professional decorum. The calibration of focus helps the company to keep striving towards its Vision & Mission.

PLH has acquire the knowledge and the know-how in drilling & related Oil & Gas services, and maintain a local experience to perform future drilling projects & related businesses on its own. PLH, through its subsidiaries, has built connection with manufacturers & organizations who can supply content / material to Oil & Gas sector and can start trading their products in said line of business.
