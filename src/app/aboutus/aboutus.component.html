ABOUT US

Petro Link Holding Company KSCC (PLH) was established in 2007. The company has an authorized capital of KD 25,000,000/-. The company has exploited the growing markets opportunity for energies by taking equity positions, acquisitions, joint ventures, and by participating in green-field projects. Since establishment, PLH has always been an opportunity seeker and actively looking for investment options in the international oil & gas sector, non-conventional oil, and alternative energy industries.

PLH has established peer to peer connection with all of its subsidiaries to maintain a professional decorum. The calibration of focus helps the company to keep striving towards its Vision & Mission.

PLH has acquire the knowledge and the know-how in drilling & related Oil & Gas services, and maintain a local experience to perform future drilling projects & related businesses on its own. PLH, through its subsidiaries, has built connection with manufacturers & organizations who can supply content / material to Oil & Gas sector and can start trading their products in said line of business.
