CK Drilling Company

CK Drilling Company (CKD) was established based on PetroLink’s strategy that focuses on taking active participation in Kuwait Development Plan.

CKD is awarded, two KOC workover contracts to participate in country’s Oil & Gas development program.

Our strategy is to have a significant role and part in KOC aggressive expansion of drilling programs for its onshore and offshore drilling activities and related oil services to help and assist in achieving and realizing their 2040 oil strategy.

http://ckdrilling.com/

KAC Trading Co

Kuwait and China Trading Company (KACT) is a subsidiary of PetroLink Holding Company.

KACT works with business partners in China to introduce, promote and sell their products and services in Kuwait. KACT portfolio ranges from Electric Cables to Transformers, Explosion Proof Motors to Gen-Sets, Pipes, Valves and Fittings to Drilling Rigs and Offshore Drilling Services.

KACT’s primary focus is Energy, Oil & Gas (EOG) related products & services, specialized in goods & services offered by KACT’s Chinese partners & international principles and to EOG sector in Kuwait.

The support KACT is receiving from its partners and its parent company (PetroLink Holding) is a key element to its exponential growth in its bid for market expansion in Kuwait.

http://kact.com/

Circle Link Limited

Circle Link S.à r.l. (hereafter the "Company") was incorporated on June 5, 2007, and is organised under the laws of Luxembourg as a Société à Responsabilité Limitée for an unlimited period. The registered office of the Company is established at 121, avenue de la Faïencerie L-1511 Luxembourg. Circle Link Limited is 100% owned subsidiary of Petrolink Holding Company.

In April 2008, PetroLink Holding Company K.S.C.C made its investment in Circle Oil by acquiring Circle Link SARL, Luxembourg (the holding SPV of Circle Oil Investment). PetroLink was entitled a 4 years convertible unsecured loan (CL) at an annual interest basis.
